My top 5 Super Powers


Find out what my super powers are, according to Gallup's® StrengthsFinder® personality test.

Of the 34 themes measured, Gallup's® Signature Themes report presents my five most dominant themes of talent, ranked in order below.

These strengths will give you an idea of how I would fit in with any team and more importantly - what strengths I can offer.


Relator

Relator describes my attitude towards relationships, pulling me toward people I already know. Once the initial connection has been made, I deliberately encourage a deepening of the relationship.

I want to understand feelings, goals, fears and dreams. For me, a relationship has value only if it is genuine and I take these steps toward real friendship willingly.

Learner

I love love love to learn. Whatever the subject, I am always drawn to the process of learning. The process is especially exciting for me as I am energized by the steady and deliberate journey from ignorance to competence.

My excitement enables me to thrive in dynamic work environments where I am asked to take on short project assignments and expected to learn a lot about the new subject matter in a short period of time and then move on to the next one.

Achiever

Achiever describes my constant need for achievement - every day starts at zero! By the end of the day I must achieve something tangible in order to feel good about myself – every day!

I have an internal fire burning inside me. It pushes me to do more, to achieve more. It is the power supply that causes me to set the pace and define the levels of productivity for my work group. It is the theme that keeps me moving.

Intellection

I love to think. I like mental activity. This need for mental activity may or may not be focused! I am the kind of person who enjoys my time alone because it is my time for musing and reflection.

I can be introspective, my own best companion, as I pose myself questions and try out answers on myself to see how they might work.

Arranger

I am a conductor. When faced with a complex situation involving many factors, I enjoy managing all of the variables, aligning and realigning them until I am sure I have arranged them in the most productive configuration possible.

I am a shining example of effective flexibility!

From the mundane to the complex, I am always looking for the perfect configuration and I am at my best in dynamic situations. I jump into the confusion, devising new options, hunting for new paths of least resistance, and figuring out new partnerships—because, after all, there might just be a better way!
