eWoof

eWoof is a family run business providing home from home services for your peace of mind.


With busy working lives and commitments we all need a helping hand.
If you can't be there to enjoy a walk with your best friend or keep them company during the day, let us help.

Whether you need care for your buddy while you take a well earned break or you can't quite trust the sofa to be in one piece when you get home, we're here to help.

Our mission is simply to provide high quality care in a safe environment, for your dog, in your absence.

Fancy a Walk?

Whether you're pooch is staying with us for the day or just needs a day time walk, eWoof loves to walk.
We walk predominantly in enclosed fields with lots of space, safely away from traffic, livestock and main roads.
Walks are generally an hour, but for our littler or more senior customers, we can take it easy too.

Most days we meet other groups and walkers so your pup will enjoy plenty of socialisation.

Many of our human customers report a more sociable, calm and confident dog after several walks with us.

Boarding

eWoof welcomes both regular and ad hoc boarders. We board a maximum of 4 guest dogs in our home at any time.

Our guests have the run of the ground floor, with access to a self-contained garden, secured by locked doors all round and 6ft fences.
Guests can snooze on heated floors or in cool rooms, inside or outside, whatever they prefer.

There are 2 walks per day, so pooches enjoy plenty of socialisation and exercise.
There is plenty of space to play or spend time alone. We look after your pet as if they were part of our family.


We welcome raw feeders and have seperate facilities for storing and preparing all food.

Day Boarding

Day boarders are very welcome either on a regular or ad hoc basis.

Our standard hours for day boarding are 8am-6pm, Monday to Friday.

Weekends may be available on request

Overnight Stays

Our overnight customers are free to sleep wherever they feel comfortable - on rugs, sofas, cool floors (or hot floors), in their own beds or a crate (which you may wish to bring with you).

Overnight stays are charged per day from 8am, with, collection before 10am the following day


eWoof is available for long-term stays so that you can enjoy a well deserved break or holiday. Your dog will enjoy a home from home experience with us and have a prety amazing holiday making new friends and getting plenty of time to play and run about - everyone's happy!
